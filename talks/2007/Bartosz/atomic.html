  • Sequencing
    • Sequencing Point: switch to READ-CHECK status
    • Decision Point: switch to SUCCESSFUL or FAILED status
    • Between these two points the system is in a limbo
  • Schroedinger's cat
    • Sequencing point: we fire the electron that may or may not release the cyanide
    • The system is in a quantum superposition of two states
    • Decision point: we open the cage and find out if the cat is alive or dead
    • If somebody tries to open the cage in between, they don't get the logical value
      • They have to kill the cat!
    • From the point of view of other threads, the transaction occurs at the sequencing point
