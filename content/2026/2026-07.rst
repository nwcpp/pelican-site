Title
~~~~~
AI for Normal People Who Aren’t Totally Bought In

Abstract
~~~~~~~~~

In this practical and demo-filled session, Scott Hanselman will cut through the AI hype to deliver a balanced perspective.
You’ll learn what’s genuinely helpful versus what’s harmful, and why we absolutely must double down on investing in junior
engineers to help them develop good taste as AI threatens to reshape software engineering completely.
