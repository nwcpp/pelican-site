Title

Open Discussion: Chatting on ChatGPT

Abstract

Generative AI is all the rage right now, in particular ChatGPT. Come join us for a lively open discussion and hopefully some live demos of this emerging technology. What is it, how does it work, what can you do with it, how much of a threat is it? For folks that haven’t played with ChatGPT yet here is the public link: https://chat.openai.com/auth/login
