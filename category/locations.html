Microsoft Meeting Room: Reactor, Building 20

Published: Tue 04 February 2025
By Peter Williamson

In locations.

Our meetings are held at the main Microsoft campus, in the Reactor Room, Building 20, at the corner of NE 31st and 156th Ave NE, in Redmond, WA.

If you use the main entrance from 156th (NE 26th St), take the first left onto Microsoft Way, and the Reactor room is the second building on the left

Usually, we meet at 7pm on the third Thursday of the month.

Reactor Room, Cafeteria 20.
Microsoft Campus,
156th Ave NE,
Redmond, WA 98052

Disclaimer: NWCPP is not associated with Microsoft—although we take advantage of their generosity in providing the venue (since 2006) and occasional speakers.
